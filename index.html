ayisha thier,

is a designer and visual artist based in Montreal. She communicates using computer-generated imagery and analog design methods. Ayisha explores techniques associated with women’s work such as knitting and felting, where materiality offers a sensory experience that is also part of a contemporary feminist discourse. Challenging capitalist structures and social order is a primary focal point for Ayisha as her creations enter the Anthropocene.


Ayisha prioritizes curatorial endeavors and collaboration with visual artists to eliminate the boundaries between art and design. Besides her own artistic practice, she handcrafts sculptural lamps for contemporary furniture studio Stamátios Fragos
